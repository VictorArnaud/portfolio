PGTBL - Team Based Learning Management Platform

Team-based learning or TBL is a collaborative learning teaching strategy that focuses on a three-step cycle:

  • Preparation: Basically the teacher will make available articles and study books that will be the preparation for the evaluation;
  • Class evaluations: The first evaluation is individual and the second in group to test the knowledge acquired in the preparation each with its respective weight;
  • Small project focused on the application: Apply all the knowledge acquired in a real context of the proposed topic.

PGTBL is a platform to manage and apply this methodology
